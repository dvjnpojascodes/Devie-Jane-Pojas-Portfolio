POLYQUEUE: A Queuing Technology System

1. Overview

The Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Institute of technology registrar as any other registrars has a lot of responsibilities. Among other things, they are responsible for dealing with the students’ and staffs’ academic and professional lives, safeguarding student data and supporting and building student-led courses. Along with this responsibility, is the problem about poor queue management system in the university. Thereby, PUP was labeled as Pila-Ulit-Pibecause of the long process of queuing in registrar. Considering all these problems. Through the POLYQUEUE- Queuing Technology System, the university registrar will not only improve their queuing service but also achieve student satisfaction because the queuing process will be faster, organized and free from congestion.

Role

UI Designer and Prototyping

Date Duration: July 13, 2022 - August 2, 2022

2. Defining the Problem and Goals

2.1 Problems

Management of Long Queues. An excessive number of people that are processing their documents sometimes comes out of hand.

Services from Registrar The university registrar services are damaged due to unmanageable queues.

Excessive Time and Money Consumption or Long Waiting Hours. Due to excessive number of people, the admin won’t be able to attend all of them and appointee’s time and effort are being wasted.


2.2 Goals

Increased the efficiency of PUP-Itech registrar. POLYQUEUE's ultimate goal is efficiency. Users want to accomplish as much as possible in the shortest amount of time possible. Increases productivity while lowering costs and workload. The POLYQUEUE will help minimize the time-consuming distractions associated with managing visitors and students throughout the day.

Reduced Waiting Periods. The combination of increased registrar efficiency and shorter, more productive contacts in a POLYQUEUE reduces the amount of time users wait for their turn.

Increase registrar’s satisfaction. Majority of registrars prefer to communicate with contented, less concerned customers, which a POLYQUEUE want to facilitate. IT will also help assure student,visitor,and PUP-Itech registrar staff happiness and reduce their complaints and frustrations to provide a pleasant queue experience. Both of these consequences can result to registrars feeling more fulfilled and connected to their professions.

4. User Interface Design

HI-FI UI: LOGIN / SIGN UP SECTION

HI-FI UI: Dashboard

The dashboard section of Polyqueue shows the current client served by the registrar. The on the line features shows the registrar the client’s details, such as its name, department, course, what it needs, and date requested to double-check the requested document before giving it to the client.

The active queue section will allow the registrar to oversee the following clients that are currently waiting in line. Furthermore, the dashboard section also shows the summary of how many queue there are today, how many missed their schedule, and how many were already served.

HI-FI UI: GUEST APPOINTMENT SECTION

The guest appointment section of Polyqueue will help registrar to oversee the upcoming appointments of clients that are not currently enrolled in PUP-Itech. This will allow them to ready the requested documents, so that on the day of appointment, registrar will not look for it, and will just lend the document. This will save the time of both the registrar and clients.

HI-FI UI: SCHOOL APPOINTMENT SECTION

The school appointment section of Polyqueue will help registrar to oversee the upcoming appointments graduating and current students of PUP-Itech. This will allow them to ready the requested documents, so that on the day of appointment, registrar will not look for it, and will just lend the document. This will save the time of both the registrar and clients.

HI-FI UI: POP-UP DETAIL

When registrar clicked the details button appointments, a pop-up detail will show containing the appointee’s name, section, student number, appointment, data of appointment, address, and contact number.

HI-FI UI: REPORT SECTION

The report section of Polyqueue provides summary report of appointments before. The registrar can filter it from guest or school appointments, or filter it by data duration. The report will show the details of appointee and whether it’s request document were processed or not. Furthermore, the reports section will also show summary of time duration of service on that day, the total queues, how many did not come or missed the queue, and how many were served.

Visuals

I combined a variety of shades of maroon, gray, and white to represent PUP-Itech’s brand. This is to let the users know that the system is by PUP when they are using it. When designing the user interface of Polyqueue, I make sure that it is direct to the point, and user will understand what are the buttons and sections are for. For typography, I utilized a bold font for headers, and use uniform font, Lato throughout the system. This allows for a cleaner and more modern feel while using the system.
